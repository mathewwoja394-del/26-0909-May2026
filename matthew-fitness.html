About Matthew

Matthew is a certified personal trainer with 8 years of experience helping people transform their bodies and minds through fitness. He has completed 12 marathons including the Nairobi Marathon, Lewa Marathon and the Cape Town Marathon. His passion is helping everyday people discover the athlete within them.
